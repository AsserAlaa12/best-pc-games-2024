Best Video Games of 2024

Discover the top video games of 2024, featuring stunning graphics, immersive gameplay, and unforgettable stories.

Alan Wake II: Night Springs

Alan Wake II: Night Springs is a compact collection of standalone stories that veer from entertainingly violent shootouts to tense stretches of survival horror. It's a must-play for fans of the series.

Another Crab's Treasure

Another Crab's Treasure is a charming and unique soulslike game with a colorful world and memorable characters. The shell-swapping mechanic adds a fresh twist to the genre.

Final Fantasy XIV: Dawntrail

Final Fantasy XIV: Dawntrail continues the epic saga with new adventures, characters, and breathtaking environments. It's a must-play for fans of the series and newcomers alike.
